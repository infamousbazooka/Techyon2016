Rules
  • • The team size can be a maximum of 3 members
  • • Each team will get 5 minutes to present their solutions
  • • Each team will get 5 minutes to present their solutions
  • • Developed Solutions should be related to the theme.
  • • DEMO / WORKING CODE IS MANDATORY.
  • • Complete reuse of college projects or open source projects available online are prohibited
Guidelines
  • • Teams need to COMPULSORILY be present at 9:30am where the Hackathon Theme will be told by the PSL Team and you get a chance to ask Questions regarding the Theme.

  • • If you are present later you will have to ask the Student coordinators of the Event regarding the Theme.

  • • Teams will also HAVE to come with their own Development Environment ie. Laptops etc.

  • • A power Strip will be handy.

  • • Internet connectivity will be provided at the Labs.

  • • Teams will have to check if their Hardware ie Laptops are compatible with the Projector BEFORE HAND.

  • • This is to ensure that there are no problems/incompatibility issues during the Presentation with the judges later.
How To
Teams will have to sign up online via the Website. Registration can also be done at the Venue on Day 1 BEFORE 10AM.
